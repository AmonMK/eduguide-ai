### Kenya's CBC-Native Student Intelligence Platform

EduGuide AI is an AI-powered early warning and intervention 
system built specifically for Kenya's Competency Based 
Curriculum (CBC) environment.

## Problem
Students are failing and dropping out at alarming rates. 
Teachers have no early warning tools. Parents don't 
understand CBC. Nobody sees the warning signs until it's too late.

## Solution
EduGuide AI predicts at-risk students early, recommends 
targeted interventions to teachers, and gives parents 
clarity on their child's progress and future pathways.
